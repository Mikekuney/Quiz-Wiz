Quiz-Wiz

High Scores

Quiz-Wiz is a timed, multiple choice quiz game

  • You'll have 30 seconds to answer as many questions as you can correctly.
  • Click on the answer that best fits given question to select your answer.
  • Correct answers will be awarded extra time!
  • Incorrect answers will result in loss of time!
  • If you answer all questions correctly before time runs out, then you will be awarded extra points!

When player is ready, click start to begin the game
